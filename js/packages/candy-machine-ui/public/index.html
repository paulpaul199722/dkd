What is Doge Kingdom?

Once upon a time, a man in the hamlet fell in love with a woman, who turned out to be the princess of the Doge Kingdom. Simultaneously, the princess embraced the man's love and desired to bring him back to the palace to live with her. However, there is a man who despises this man so much that he has enlisted the help of his followers to kidnap the princess. After hearing about the incident from others, the man set off on a quest to save the princess...

After overcoming all obstacles in his path, he finally made up to the top floor and successfully saved the princess. He also became the King of Doge Kingdom at the same time. They discovered that others had depleted the national treasury entirely when they thought their adventure was over. As a result, they sought Elon Musk's assistance to resurrect the Doge Kingdom's most magnificent period.Soon after, the princess gave birth to many doges, each with its own set of features. The Doge Kingdom has become quite cheerful and energetic since then...
